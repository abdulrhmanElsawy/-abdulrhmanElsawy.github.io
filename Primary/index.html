About us

Zamil Industrial

Founded in 1998 and headquartered in Dammam, Saudi Arabia, Zamil Industrial Investment Company (Zamil Industrial) is a premier business group engaged in the development of innovative design and engineering solutions for use in the construction industry.

Through its various industrial sectors, Zamil Industrial provides a broad range of products and solutions that meet the increasing needs and requirements of the global building and construction industry.
